Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2026
This Privacy Policy explains how Eagles on the Air ("Eagles on the Air," "we," "our," or "us"), the student broadcast program of Fort Bend Christian Academy ("FBCA"), handles information in connection with the FBCA.tv website, our Roku channel, and our iOS app (together, the "Services"). It applies to all three unless a section says otherwise. By using the Services, you agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Services.

The Services are operated in the United States and are intended for viewers in the United States and Canada. Our purpose is simple: to let families, students, alumni, and fans watch FBCA sports and school events, view our broadcast schedule, and reach our archives.

The Short Version

  • We do not require you to create an account or log in to watch.
  • We do not ask you for personal information such as your name or address to use the Services.
  • We use anonymous, aggregate analytics to understand how many people watch and which pages are popular — not to identify you personally.
  • Our iOS app may offer optional push notifications; you choose whether to turn these on.
  • Any information we do collect stays within FBCA and its service providers. We do not sell your information.

Information We Collect

Information you provide

The Services are built for viewing, not for submitting personal information. We do not require accounts, logins, or registration. If you choose to contact us directly (for example, by emailing us), we will receive whatever information you include in that message, and we will use it only to respond to you.

Information collected automatically (analytics)

Like most websites and streaming apps, our Services and our trusted analytics providers automatically collect limited technical information to help us understand usage and improve the experience. This may include general device or browser type, approximate region, pages or streams viewed, and the date and time of access. We use this information in aggregate and anonymous form. We do not use it to build a personal profile of you or to identify you individually.

Cookies and similar technologies (website)

Our website may use cookies — small files stored by your browser — to support analytics and remember basic preferences. You can set your browser to warn you about cookies or to refuse them; see your browser's Help menu for instructions. If you disable cookies, some features of the website may not work as intended.

Push notifications (iOS app)

Our iOS app may offer push notifications — for example, to alert you that a broadcast is about to begin. Push notifications are optional. Your device will ask for your permission before any are sent, and you can turn them off at any time in your device settings or within the app. To deliver notifications, we rely on Apple's push notification service, which uses an anonymous device token rather than your name or contact information.

How We Use Information

  • To operate, deliver, and maintain the Services, including streaming video and showing our schedule.
  • To understand, in aggregate, how the Services are used so we can improve them.
  • To send optional push notifications you have chosen to receive.
  • To respond to messages you send us.
  • To protect the Services, enforce our policies, and comply with the law.

How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. Any information collected through the Services is used by Eagles on the Air and Fort Bend Christian Academy. We may share limited information with service providers who help us operate the Services — for example, our website host, streaming provider, and analytics provider — and only so they can perform their work on our behalf. We may also disclose information where necessary to comply with the law, respond to lawful requests, enforce our policies, or protect the rights, property, or safety of our students, our school, our viewers, or the public.

Advertising

The Services do not currently display advertising. If that changes in the future, we will update this Policy before advertising begins, and we will describe what any advertising partners collect and how you can exercise any available choices. We will not introduce advertising that identifies individual minors.

Children's Privacy

Eagles on the Air is a student broadcast program, and our broadcasts and archives feature Fort Bend Christian Academy students, including minors, participating in school athletics and events.

We take children's privacy seriously. The Services are intended as a general-audience viewing platform for the school community. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, and we do not require anyone — including children — to provide personal information to watch.

Students appear in our broadcasts as participants in school-sanctioned athletic and school events, consistent with Fort Bend Christian Academy's own policies and consent practices for student participation and media. Questions from parents or guardians about a student's appearance in a broadcast or archive — including requests regarding a specific recording — should be directed to us using the contact information below, and we will work with the school to respond.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information without appropriate consent, please contact us and we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

Your Choices

  • Cookies: control or disable them through your browser settings.
  • Push notifications: turn them on or off in your device settings or within the iOS app.
  • Analytics: many browsers and devices offer "Do Not Track," ad-tracking limits, or similar controls that you may use.
  • Questions or requests: contact us anytime using the details below.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information handled through the Services. However, no method of transmission or storage over the Internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please use caution when using the Internet.

Third-Party Services and Links

The Services rely on and may link to third-party platforms — for example, our video streaming provider, the Roku platform, the Apple App Store, and our YouTube archive and social media pages. Those platforms have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. This Policy does not cover the practices of companies we do not own or control.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version here and revise the effective date above. Your continued use of the Services after an update means you accept the revised Policy.
